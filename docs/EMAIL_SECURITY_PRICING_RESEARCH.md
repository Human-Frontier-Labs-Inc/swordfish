# Email Security SaaS Pricing Research: SMB/MSP Market

## Executive Summary

This research analyzes the email security SaaS market pricing for SMB and MSP channels, covering major competitors, pricing structures, and strategic recommendations for a new market entrant.

**Key Findings:**
- SMB per-user pricing ranges from **$2.00 to $6.00/user/month**
- Enterprise pricing ranges from **$4.00 to $8.00+/user/month**
- MSP wholesale margins typically range from **30-50%** off list price
- PLG (Product-Led Growth) is gaining traction but security products often require hybrid PLG+sales approach
- Stripe is viable for B2B SaaS but Merchant of Record solutions (Paddle, FastSpring) simplify tax/compliance

## 2. SMB vs Enterprise Pricing Differences

### SMB Characteristics (< 250 users)
- **Price sensitivity**: High - budget is primary concern
- **Decision maker**: Often owner/IT generalist, not security specialist
- **Typical spend**: $2.50 - $4.00/user/month
- **Contract length**: Monthly to annual
- **Self-service**: Preferred when possible

### Enterprise Characteristics (250+ users)
- **Price sensitivity**: Lower - ROI and features matter more
- **Decision maker**: CISO, security team, procurement
- **Typical spend**: $4.00 - $8.00+/user/month
- **Contract length**: 1-3 years with auto-renewal
- **Negotiation**: Expected; 5-25% discounts common
- **Custom requirements**: SLAs, compliance, integrations

### Volume Discount Structure (Typical)
| User Count | Discount Range |
|------------|---------------|
| 1-49 | List price |
| 50-249 | 10-15% off |
| 250-999 | 15-25% off |
| 1000+ | 25-40% off |

## 3. MSP Partner Pricing Structures

### Common MSP Pricing Models

#### A. Wholesale/Reseller Model
- **Typical margin**: 30-50% off MSRP
- **Billing**: Monthly or annual to MSP, MSP bills customer
- **Examples**: TitanHQ, Hornetsecurity, Barracuda MSP

#### B. Tiered Partner Programs
Most vendors use tiered partner programs with escalating benefits:

| Tier | Typical Discount | Requirements |
|------|-----------------|--------------|
| Registered | 20-30% | Minimal; sign agreement |
| Silver | 30-40% | Revenue commitment, training |
| Gold | 40-50% | Higher revenue, certifications |
| Platinum | 50%+ | Strategic accounts, volume |

#### C. NFR (Not-For-Resale) Programs
- Free or deeply discounted internal use for MSPs
- Guardz: Free Community tier for MSPs
- Mesh Security: Internal use free with paying customers

### Vendor-Specific MSP Programs

**TitanHQ/SpamTitan:**
- "Generous margins" (not publicly disclosed)
- White-label capability
- Monthly billing aligned with MSP cycles
- No startup fees or price hikes
- Base price from $1.95/user/month

**Guardz:**
- Community tier: Free
- Pro tier: ~$5/user/month
- Claims 75% cost savings vs. multiple tools
- Unified platform approach

**IRONSCALES (via Syncro partnership):**
- Recently partnered with Syncro for MSP distribution
- Tiered pricing with volume discounts
- Free tier available for training features

**Proofpoint Essentials:**
- Standard Price List available
- MSP-specific pricing through channel

### MSP Margin Expectations
Based on industry feedback:
- **Minimum viable margin**: 25-30%
- **Healthy margin target**: 40-50%
- **Top performers**: 50%+

MSPs typically mark up 30-50% from wholesale to end customer, meaning:
- If wholesale = $2.50/user, customer pays $3.50-$4.00/user
- If wholesale = $3.00/user, customer pays $4.00-$5.00/user

## 4. Feature-Based Tiering Patterns

### Standard Three-Tier Model

#### Basic/Starter Tier ($2-3/user/month)
- Spam filtering
- Basic malware detection
- Quarantine management
- Basic reporting
- Standard support

#### Pro/Advanced Tier ($3-5/user/month)
Everything in Basic, plus:
- Advanced threat protection (ATP)
- BEC/impersonation detection
- URL rewriting/sandboxing
- Enhanced reporting/analytics
- API access
- Priority support

Everything in Pro, plus:
- Email archiving
- DLP (Data Loss Prevention)

## 5. PLG vs Sales-Led Pricing Differences

### Product-Led Growth (PLG) Characteristics

**Pricing Approach:**
- Transparent, published pricing
- Free tier or free trial (freemium model)
- Self-service signup and onboarding
- Usage-based or per-seat pricing
- Low friction to start
- Monthly billing options

**Examples in Security:**
- IRONSCALES (free training tier)
- Guardz (free Community tier)
- Atlassian model ($10 starter licenses)

**PLG Metrics:**
- CAC (Customer Acquisition Cost): Lower than sales-led
- Time to value: Hours/days vs. weeks/months
- Conversion rate: 2-5% free to paid typical
- Expansion revenue: Critical for unit economics

### Sales-Led Growth (SLG) Characteristics

**Pricing Approach:**
- "Contact us for pricing"
- Custom quotes required
- Annual contracts expected
- Negotiation expected
- High-touch onboarding
- Enterprise-focused

**Examples in Security:**
- Abnormal Security
- Mimecast
- Enterprise tiers of most vendors

**SLG Metrics:**
- Higher ACV (Annual Contract Value)
- Longer sales cycles (weeks to months)
- Higher CAC but higher LTV
- Land and expand strategy

### Hybrid Model (Recommended for Email Security)

Most successful email security companies use a **hybrid PLG + SLG approach**:

| Segment | Approach | Price Point |
|---------|----------|-------------|
| SMB (<50 users) | PLG self-service | $3-5/user |
| Mid-market (50-500) | PLG + inside sales | $3-4/user |
| Enterprise (500+) | Sales-led | Custom |

**Why hybrid for security:**
- Security requires trust (sales helps build)
- Compliance requirements need discussion
- But PLG reduces CAC for high-volume SMB
- Free trials prove value before commitment

### PLG Statistics (2025)
- 58% of SaaS businesses have PLG model
- 91% plan to increase PLG investments
- 77% of $250M+ ARR SaaS use some PLG
- 65% of buyers prefer both PLG and sales experiences

## 6. Payment Processors for B2B SaaS

### Stripe

**Pros:**
- Best-in-class developer experience
- Extensive documentation and APIs
- Strong subscription billing (Stripe Billing)
- Global payment method support
- Revenue recognition tools

**Cons:**
- Not a Merchant of Record (you handle tax/compliance)
- Transaction fees: 2.9% + $0.30 (US)
- Need additional tools for invoicing, tax
- Compliance burden remains with you

**Best for:** Companies with engineering resources to build billing infrastructure

### Merchant of Record (MoR) Alternatives

| Provider | Fee | Best For |
|----------|-----|----------|
| **Paddle** | 5% + $0.50 | SaaS recurring billing, global sales tax |
| **FastSpring** | ~5-6% | Digital goods, software licensing |
| **Lemon Squeezy** | 5% + $0.50 | Indie SaaS, simplicity |
| **Dodo** | Varies | AI/usage-based billing |

**MoR Benefits:**
- Handle VAT/GST in 200+ countries
- Fraud protection included
- No separate tax software needed
- Simplify international expansion

### B2B-Specific Solutions

| Provider | Focus | Notes |
|----------|-------|-------|
| **Maxio** | B2B SaaS billing | Usage-based + subscriptions |
| **Chargebee** | Subscription management | Enterprise-grade |
| **Cacheflow** | B2B checkout | Quote-to-cash |
| **Hyperline** | Billing orchestration | Integrates with Stripe |

### Recommendation by Stage

| Stage | Recommendation | Reason |
|-------|---------------|--------|
| Pre-revenue | Stripe | Fast setup, developer-friendly |
| $0-$1M ARR | Stripe or Paddle | Balance flexibility and simplicity |
| $1M-$10M ARR | Paddle/MoR | Tax compliance becomes critical |
| $10M+ ARR | Custom stack | Maxio/Chargebee + Stripe |

### B2B-Specific Considerations
- **Invoicing**: Many B2B customers want invoices, not card payments
- **Net terms**: Net 30/60/90 common in enterprise
- **PO numbers**: Required by procurement
- **Tax exemptions**: B2B sales often exempt (need verification)

## 7. Recommended Pricing Strategy for New Entrant

### Target Market Position
Position as **"Modern, SMB-first email security"** with competitive pricing and superior user experience.

### Recommended Pricing Structure

#### Free Tier (PLG Lead Generation)
- **Price**: $0
- **Users**: Up to 10 mailboxes
- **Features**: Basic spam/phishing, limited retention
- **Purpose**: Build awareness, generate leads

#### Starter Tier (SMB Self-Service)
- **Price**: $2.50/user/month (billed monthly) or $2.00/user/month (billed annually)
- **Minimum**: 5 users
- **Features**:
  - Advanced spam filtering
  - AI phishing detection
  - Quarantine management
  - Basic reporting
  - Email support

#### Professional Tier (Growth SMB/MSP)
- **Price**: $4.00/user/month (billed monthly) or $3.50/user/month (billed annually)
- **Minimum**: 10 users
- **Features** (everything in Starter, plus):
  - BEC protection
  - URL sandboxing
  - Advanced threat intelligence
  - API access
  - Priority support
  - Basic automation

#### Business Tier (Mid-Market)
- **Price**: $6.00/user/month (billed monthly) or $5.00/user/month (billed annually)
- **Minimum**: 25 users
- **Features** (everything in Professional, plus):
  - Email archiving (90 days)
  - DLP policies
  - Advanced automation
  - Custom rules engine
  - SSO/SAML
  - Dedicated support

#### Enterprise Tier (Sales-Led)
- **Price**: Custom (starting ~$4.50/user/month at volume)
- **Features** (everything in Business, plus):
  - Unlimited archiving
  - Security Awareness Training
  - Account takeover protection
  - Custom integrations
  - SLA guarantees
  - Dedicated account manager
  - Compliance certifications

### MSP Partner Pricing

| MSP Tier | Discount | Requirements |
|----------|----------|--------------|
| Partner | 30% | Agreement, 25+ seats |
| Silver | 40% | 100+ seats, training |
| Gold | 50% | 500+ seats, certifications |

**MSP-Specific Features:**
- Multi-tenant management console
- White-label options (Gold+)
- Monthly billing alignment
- NFR licenses for internal use
- Co-marketing support

### Pricing Rationale

1. **Below-market Starter price ($2.50)**: Undercuts Barracuda ($2.66), competitive with SpamTitan ($1.95)
2. **Mid-market Professional ($4.00)**: Matches Avanan/IRONSCALES territory
3. **Value-packed Business ($6.00)**: Competes with Mimecast/enterprise features at SMB price
4. **Enterprise flexibility**: Enables deal negotiation for large accounts

### Go-to-Market Recommendations

1. **Start with PLG** for SMB segment:
   - Free trial (14 days) + free tier (10 mailboxes)
   - Self-service onboarding
   - Transparent pricing page

2. **Build MSP channel** for scale:
   - Generous margins (40-50%)
   - Partner portal with billing tools
   - White-label capability

3. **Add sales motion** for mid-market:
   - Inside sales team at $5M+ ARR
   - Focus on 50-500 user accounts
   - Annual contracts with discounts

4. **Payment infrastructure**:
   - Start with Stripe for simplicity
   - Add Paddle/MoR for international expansion
   - Implement invoicing for enterprise
